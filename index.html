Product Overview

What is ROFLnator Studio?

A purpose-built workflow tool for creators and media brands managing short-form video at scale.

Built for Creator Workflows

ROFLnator Studio is a creator workflow tool that helps creators and media brands organize short-form video assets, connect supported social accounts, review content queues, and send approved content into publishing flows.

Instead of juggling files across cloud drives, spreadsheets, and native apps, ROFLnator Studio gives you a single dashboard to manage the entire lifecycle of your short-form content—from asset ingest to published post.

The tool is operated by JOGEE Consulting LLC and designed for creators who need a reliable, no-nonsense workflow layer between their content library and their publishing destinations.

Key Features

Everything You Need to Ship Short-Form Content

Four core capabilities designed to eliminate the friction between your content library and your audience.

Connect Supported Social Accounts

Securely link your TikTok, Instagram, and Threads accounts via official OAuth flows. Manage multiple accounts per platform from one dashboard with granular permission control.

Review Queued Short-Form Content

Centralized review queue for all pending videos. Watch full-screen previews, read captions, verify hashtags, and approve or reject with one click. Keyboard shortcuts for power users.

Upload Approved Assets to Publishing Flows

Push approved videos directly into supported publishing flows with captions, hashtags, and scheduling metadata intact. No manual re-entry. Draft, schedule, or publish immediately.

Unified Publishing Status Dashboard

Real-time status grid showing every asset across Draft, Queued, Approved, Publishing, Published, and Failed states. Filter by platform, account, date range, or team member.

Secure Account Management

OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for all platform connections. Tokens encrypted at rest. Revoke access instantly from the dashboard. No passwords ever stored.

Team Collaboration Ready

Invite team members with role-based permissions (Admin, Editor, Viewer). Activity log tracks every approval, rejection, and publish action. Comments on assets for feedback loops.

Workflow

How It Works in 4 Steps

From raw footage to published post—ROFLnator Studio streamlines every handoff.

1

Ingest Assets

Drag and drop short-form videos, captions, and hashtag sets into your asset library. Organize with tags, folders, and custom metadata fields.

2

Connect Accounts

Link TikTok, Instagram, and Threads accounts via official OAuth. Manage multiple accounts per platform. Permissions scoped to publishing only.

3

Review & Approve

Queue assets for review. Preview full-screen, verify captions and tags, approve or request changes. Collaborate with team comments.

4

Publish & Track

Push approved content to publishing flows. Schedule or post immediately. Monitor status from Draft to Published in the unified dashboard.
